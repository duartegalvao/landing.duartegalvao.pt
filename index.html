Computer Engineering

MsC alumnus from Instituto Superior Técnico (IST) of the University of Lisbon. Worked as a Teaching Assistant for Computer Architecture and Databases courses.

Coordinated the collaboration between Indico and JACoW, attending large conferences such as IPAC to perform user-tests in real-life scenarios and develop improvements to the tools together with its users.

Currently working as an independent contractor developing web platforms focused on event management and organizing.
